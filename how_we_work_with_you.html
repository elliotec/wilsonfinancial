How We Work With You

Initial Evaluation

We offer a free half-hour consultation to acquaint you with our services and give you an estimate of the cost. We give no advice during the first meeting. The concept of the initial conference is to allow potential clients a chance to assess our capabilities and determine if we are a good fit.

Hourly and Project Work

  • Hourly rates and project costs are set by each planner and usually accompany a timeline.
  • All charges are billed in arrears.
  • The client is proactive and responsible for identifying the body of work and specific goals.
  • The planner does not monitor accounts on a regular basis.
  • Many clients choose hourly or project work if they have questions regarding a specific financial issue, desire a second opinion or require a regular “check-up” on one or more aspects of their financial life.

Retainer and Contract Clients

  • Clients receive comprehensive financial planning and wealth management services on an annual basis.
  • The responsibility falls on the advisor to make timely suggestions and provide regular guidance on every aspect of the client’s financial well-being.
  • Typically, contract or retainer clients are interested in a long-term planning relationship.
  • The contract fee takes into consideration the complexity of the work and the amount of assets under management.
  • We do not use an AUM style to calculate the contract value. Contract fees are not calculated on the fluctuating value of the portfolio or performance.
