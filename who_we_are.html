Who We Are

Kent L. Wilson, CFP®, CPA,
President

Kent is a Certified Public Accountant, a Certified Financial Planner™, and a NAPFA-Registered Financial Advisor. Kent graduated from Utah State University with a B.S. degree in accounting. Prior to joining Wilson Financial Advisors in 1994, he was with a CPA firm for eight years, giving him an extensive background in tax planning. Kent has been quoted in Smart Money magazine and the Wall Street Journal. He was recognized as being one of the top 150 financial planners in the nation in 2000, 2002, 2004, 2006 and 2008 by Medical Economics magazine.

Thomas Fritz, CFP®,
Vice President

Thomas is a Certified Financial Planner™ and NAPFA-Registered Financial Advisor. Thomas holds a B.S. degree in business management from Brigham Young University, Hawaii. With over 25 years of experience, he specializes in investment analysis and portfolio strategies, including maximization of diversification to reduce risk. Thomas designs and presents educational seminars at professional organizations and for clients on a variety of financial planning and investment topics. He also works with 401(k) plans to educate participants about sound investment principles. Thomas has been interviewed many times by local and national media about the financial planning profession and current topics of interest.

Kenneth R. Poulsen, CFP®


Ken is a Certified Financial Planner™ professional with a B.A. in Banking and Financial Services from Columbia College. Ken joined Wilson Financial Advisors in 2010 after spending time as a financial advisor with Edward Jones Investments. He has been interviewed by local media about a variety of financial planning and investment topics. While continuing to build his client base, he assists both partners of the firm with the day to day planning and investment duties of the firm’s clients.
